What we get asked most.

What does 'Signature' mean?+
Signature is Lawang's fully turnkey product line. You receive a completed villa — permitted, built, and ready to move in or rent out — together with the legal structure (PT PMA + HGB freehold title). It is the complete solution, not a build-it-yourself kit. Everything from land acquisition to front-door key is handled by our team.
Is it for living or for investing?+
Both. Every Signature villa is designed for premium private living and for competitive rental returns. Many owners split their time — staying several weeks a year and renting for the rest through our managed programme. The freehold structure means the asset is yours in the way a primary residence would be.
Where exactly is it?+
Balian Hills, on the west coast of Bali — one of the island's most privileged residential zones. 15 minutes from Canggu, 2 minutes from Amazona Jungle Resort, and 6 minutes from the British school. The area combines jungle calm with access to Bali's most dynamic social corridor.
What type of ownership do I get?+
HGB Freehold title, held through your own PT PMA (Indonesian limited company). This is the strongest form of legal ownership available to foreign investors in Indonesia — not a 30-year lease, not a nominee arrangement, but a registered title in a company you own. Our legal team handles the full PT PMA formation process for approximately €1,000.
Can I buy as a foreign national?+
Yes. Foreign nationals cannot hold property in Indonesia under their personal name — but they can own it legally through an Indonesian company (PT PMA) that they control 100%. Lawang coordinates the full PT PMA formation: registration, legal documentation and ongoing compliance support. This is the standard structure used by thousands of international investors in Bali.
How does the purchase process work?+
1. Discovery call — we understand your goals and confirm availability. 2. Reservation — a deposit locks your plot while you complete due diligence. 3. PT PMA + contract — legal structure formed, HGB title process begins. 4. Construction — steel frame build with monthly photo updates. 5. Handover — complete documentation, snagging sign-off, and rental manager introduction. First rental income typically within 90 days of handover.
What returns can I expect?+
We do not provide guaranteed yield figures. Historically, well-managed design-led villas on Bali's west coast have achieved 75–85% occupancy in high season, with average daily rates that have tracked tourism demand.
