The investment case

Why Indonesia. Why Balian. Why now.

Real ownership — not leasehold
FREEHOLD HGB · PT PMA

Most Bali properties are sold on leasehold (30+15 years). Lawang operates through HGB freehold titles held via your own PT PMA company. You own the land. Not a lease — the actual asset. This is the single most important differentiator in the Bali investment market today.

An emerging zone

Balian Hills sits 15 minutes west of Canggu — the fastest-growing zone in Bali. Where Canggu is saturated, Balian still has horizon. Land prices are rising as infrastructure catches up. Early ownership here is the same position Canggu buyers had ten years ago.

Architectural value

Lawang villas are built on steel frames with locally sourced tropical materials — designed for durability, cross-ventilation, and the kind of indoor-outdoor living that commands premium rental rates. A property built to last, in a market that rewards design.

Yield and lifestyle

Bali's tourist arrivals reached record levels in 2024, with structural undersupply in design-led stays. Historically, well-positioned villas in the west coast corridor have achieved occupancy rates above 80% in high season. Managed by our partner operator from day one.

Questions

What we get asked most.

What type of ownership do I get?+
HGB Freehold title, held through your own PT PMA (Indonesian limited company). This is the strongest form of legal ownership available to foreign investors in Indonesia — not a 30-year lease, but a registered title in a company you own. Our legal team handles the full PT PMA formation process for approximately €1,000.
Can I buy as a foreign national?+
Yes. Foreign nationals cannot hold property in Indonesia under their personal name — but they can own it legally through an Indonesian company (PT PMA) that they control 100%. Lawang coordinates the full PT PMA formation: registration, legal documentation and ongoing compliance support. This is the standard structure used by thousands of international investors in Bali.
Is it for living or for investing?+
Both. Every Lawang property is designed for premium private living and for competitive rental returns. Many owners split their time — staying several weeks a year and renting for the rest through our managed programme. The freehold structure means the asset is yours the way a primary residence would be.
How does the purchase process work?+
1. Discovery call — we understand your goals and confirm availability. 2. Reservation — a deposit locks your plot while you complete due diligence. 3. PT PMA + contract — legal structure formed, HGB title process begins. 4. Construction — steel frame build with monthly photo updates. 5. Handover — complete documentation, snagging sign-off, and rental manager introduction. First rental income typically within 90 days of handover.
Where exactly is Balian Hills?+
On the west coast of Bali — 15 minutes from Canggu, 2 minutes from Amazona Jungle Resort, and 6 minutes from Best Sun School. The area combines jungle calm with access to Bali's most dynamic social corridor. It is where Canggu was a decade ago.
What returns can I expect?+
We do not provide guaranteed yield figures. Historically, well-managed design-led villas on Bali's west coast have achieved 75–85% occupancy in high season, with average daily rates that have tracked tourism demand. Specific rental projections for each property are shared during our investor call. All figures are estimates based on historical performance — not a guarantee of future returns.
Do you also have land for sale?+
Yes. Titled land parcels are available in both Balian Hills (Bali) and Sumba. They come with full due diligence, road access, and utility connections to the boundary. You can build using a Lawang typology or your own architect. See the full portfolio at the link below.
